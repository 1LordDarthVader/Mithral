Character Creation

Ability Score Determination

Unless otherwise stated, players roll for stats. The method used is 4d6, drop lowest, reroll 1s and 2s. Roll a number of times equal to 1 + the number of ability scores you use, and drop the lowest of the rolls, then assign the rolls however you wish.

Starting Equipment

Unless otherwise stated, use the starting equipment from your class and background, don't roll for starting wealth.

In addition, characters may choose to receive any equipment pack as the equipment pack in their starting equipment, instead of the one normally granted by their class.
