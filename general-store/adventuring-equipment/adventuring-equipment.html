Adventuring Equipment

Adventurer's Pack

A character may buy an item called an adventurer's pack. It weighs 20 lbs and costs 100 gp.

The item has 50 charges, and you may use your action to expend a charge pull out any single item from the following categories, subtracting the item's cost in gp from the number of charges, even this would leave you with a number of charges that is not a whole number.
  • Animal Equipment
  • Armor
  • Consumables
  • Containers
  • Gaming Sets
  • General Equipment
  • Magical Foci
  • Musical Instrument
  • Trade Goods
  • Tools
  • Simple and Martial Weapons (but not Firearms)
Buying multiple of this item will give you one instance of the item with a higher number of charges, but the same weight.
