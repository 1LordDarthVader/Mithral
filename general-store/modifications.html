Modifications

A given suit of armor can only have one modification, as they are differing materials.
Name Light Cost Medium Cost Heavy Cost Description
Adamantine x3 x5 Any critical hit against you becomes a normal hit
Dragonsteel x2 x4 You gain resistance to the damage type of the breath weapon of the dragon type used to craft the dragonsteel used
Mithral x6 x10 If the armor has a Strength requirement, that requirement is removed; if the armor gives disadvantage on stealth, it no longer does
Name Cost Description
Aerodynamic Edge 100 gp The shield gains the Thrown (20/60) and Returning properties. Tower shields are too unwieldy to benefit from this modification.
Shield Sconce 10 gp A torch or lantern can be attached to the shield.
Shield Sling 5 gp With a sling attached (and being worn), the time it takes to don/doff a shield is reduced to an object interaction instead of an action. A buckler is too small to benefit from this modification.
Shield Spikes 50 gp When attacking with the shield, you can choose to deal piercing damage instead of bludgeoning damage .
Razor Edge 50 gp When attacking with the shield, you can choose to deal slashing damage instead of bludgeoning damage .
A given weapon can only have one modification, as they are differing materials.
Name Cost Description
Cold Iron 50 gp The weapon ignores certain resistances and immunities, such as those on celestials, fey, and fiends.
Alchemical Silver 100 gp The weapon ignores certain resistances and immunities, such as those on lycanthropes and undead.
Dragonsteel 100 gp The weapon deals an extra 1d4 damage of the damage type of the primary breath weapon of the dragon type used to craft the dragonsteel used.
Adamantine 500 gp The weapon ignores certain resistances and immunities, such as those on constructs, and deals double damage to objects.
Mithral 1000 gp The weapon gains the light property if it does not have the two-handed or heavy properties, or loses the heavy property if it has it.
