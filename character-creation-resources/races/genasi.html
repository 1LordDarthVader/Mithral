Genasi

Those who think of other planes at all consider them remote, distant realms, but planar influence can be felt throughout the world. It sometimes manifests in beings who, through an accident of birth, carry the power of the planes in their blood. The genasi are one such people, the offspring of genies and mortals.

Genasi Features

Ability Score Increase. Your Constitution score increases by 2.

Age. Genasi mature at about the same rate as humans and reach adulthood in their late teens. They live somewhat longer than humans do, up to 120 years.

Alignment. Independent and self-reliant, genasi tend toward a neutral alignment.

Size. Genasi are as varied as their mortal parents but are generally built like humans, standing anywhere from 5 feet to over 6 feet tall. Your size is Medium.

Speed. Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Innate Spellcasting. You know the a cantrip. Once you reach 3rd level, you can cast the a 1st level spell once using this trait, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. Once you reach 5th level, you can cast a 2nd level spell, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for these spells. The specific spells are determined by your subrace, as shown below.

Elemental Genasi

Air Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity score increases by 1.

Lightweight. You are immune to falling damage.

Earth Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength score increases by 1.

Digger. You have a burrow speed of 10 feet, which you can use to burrow through soil, sand, or other soft materials. Any tunnels you make while burrowing collapse behind you. You cannot end your turn underground.

Fire Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

Fire Resistance. You are resistant to fire damage.

Water Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

Amphibious. You can breathe air as well as water and have a swimming speed of 30 feet.

Para-elemental Genasi

Ice Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity or Wisdom score increases by 1.

Cold Resistance. You are resistant to cold damage.

Magma Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength or Intelligence score increases by 1.

Lava Walk. You can move across lava as if it was a solid surface and do not take damage from doing so.

Ooze Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength or Wisdom score increases by 1.

Acid Resistance. You are resistant to acid damage.

Smoke Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity or Intelligence score increases by 1.

Filtered Breath. You are immune to airborne toxins.

Quasi-elemental Genasi

Ash Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Intelligence or Charisma score increases by 1.

Filtered Breath. You are immune to airborne toxins.

Dust Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength or Charisma score increases by 1.

Filtered Breath. You are immune to airborne toxins.

Lightning Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity or Charisma score increases by 1.

Lightning Resistance. You are resistant to lightning damage.

Mineral Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength or Charisma score increases by 1.

Jeweler. You have proficiency with jeweler's tools and you have advantage on checks made to determine the value of gemstones.

Radiance Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Intelligence or Charisma score increases by 1.

Radiant Resistance. You are resistant to radiant damage.

Salt Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Wisdom or Charisma score increases by 1.

Indifference to Water. You do not need to drink.

Steam Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Intelligence or Charisma score increases by 1.

Piercing Gaze. You can see perfectly through fog, smoke, steam, and similar airborne concealments.

Vacuum Genasi

Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity or Charisma score increases by 1.

Indifference to Air. You do not need to breathe.
