Aarakocra

Sequestered in high mountains atop tall trees, the aarakocra evoke fear and wonder. Many of these birdfolk hail from the boundless vistas of the Elemental Plane of Air. They are immigrants, refugees, scouts, and explorers, their outposts functioning as footholds in a world both strange and alien.

Aarakocra Features

Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

Age. Aarakocra reach maturity by age 3. Compared to humans, aarakocra don't usually live longer than 30 years, though they can hypothetically live up to 70 years.

Alignment. Most aarakocra are good and rarely choose sides when it comes to law and chaos. Tribal leaders and warriors might be lawful, while explorers and adventurers might tend toward chaotic.

Size. The Aarakocra range from about 3 to 5 feet tall. They have thin, lightweight bodies that weigh between 50 and 100 pounds and sizes ranging from Small to Medium.

Speed. Your base walking speed is 25 feet.

Flight. You have a flying speed of 50 feet. To fly, you can't be wearing medium or heavy armor.

Language. You can speak, read, and write Common, Aarakocra, and Auran

Subraces

Alia'eet

To most other races, the Alia'eet are reported to be the only known subrace of Aarakocra, they make up the entirety of their military might. Their scouts and fighters are seen almost exclusively with all encounters with other races. They are typically the only subrace of the Aarakocra that bleed into the material plane, and as such, they are easily mistaken as the only Aarakocra archetype to exist within texts, eyewitness reports and lore books.

Appearance. They are commonly mistaken for eagles given the height that they fly, with white feathered heads and strong curled yellow beaks. The core of their bodies are brown plumage with some extremities containing more white feathers like their posterior crest.

Size. Alia'eet are around 4-5 feet high, your size is Medium.

Ability Score Increase. Your Strength score increases by 1.

Vicious Talons. Your talons are particularly vicious, which deal 1d8 of slashing damage when you attack with them.

Eagles Scream. As a bonus action, you let out an intimidatingly shrill scream at a target within 60 ft. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw with a DC 8 + your Strength modifier + your proficiency bonus. On a failure, the target is frightened of you until the start of your next turn. You can use this feature once, and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest.

Bubo Vir

The wise sages of the aarakocra family, these birdfolk are seen as the elders of their tribes. Often they are skilled healers, well versed in the druidic arts and have a quiet respect for nature. The Bubo Vir convey themselves with a proud sense of decorum when conducting their affairs. Not much can get under their feathers, always seemingly calm and collected, even within times of duress.

Appearance. Bubo Vir have tawny plumage, flat pale feathered faces, prominent ear-tufts and barred plumage that distinguishes them from other Aarakocra. They are slightly smaller compared to other birdfolk and also have large yellow eyes and a short black curled beak.

Size. Bubo Vir are 3-4 feet high, your size is Small.

Ability Score Increase. Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

Talons. You are proficient with your unarmed strikes using your hands or feet, which deal 1d6 slashing damage on a hit.

Darkvision. You are able to see in the dark, up to a range of 60 feet in shades of grey.

Naturally Perceptive. Being able to rotate your head almost completely around, you are naturally proficient in the Perception skill.

Anocetta

Colorful, upbeat and driven, the Anocetta are known for keeping spirits high, expressing their inner soul to provide entertainment and boosting morale within Aarakocra culture. If something is colorful, musical or artistic, the equally expressive Anocetta are sure to let their boastful presence be known. They are regularly patrolling to be the life of the party or vying for attention, and can sometimes be garrulous in their ways, but they are eager to please and see cheering-up others as a challenge to always accept.

Appearance. Anocetta are predominantly blue with a white chest and underparts, and a blue crest. They have a black, U-shaped collar around the neck and a black border behind their crest. They can also be found in an assortment of other colors ranging in red, orange and green, sometimes being darker or more mute in females.

Size. Anocetta are 3-4 feet high, your size is Small.

Ability Score Increase. Your Charisma score increases by 1.

Birdsong. Your natural morning birdsong can bestow tranquility to those around you. After a long rest you can choose one ally within 60ft. to gain advantage for their first roll of the day. This must be done once in the morning and lasts until mid-day.

Natural Melody. You are a natural when it comes to creating songs and tunes at a moments notice. You are proficient in the Performance skill.

Tarius

The Tarius are known to be skilled runners with very capable ground movement, and capable fighters able to exploit enemies when they are knocked down. They are skittish and quick to run, but typically it is towards the direction of danger not away. Tarius are eager to put a plan to action, usually as there is never a plan to begin with, getting frustrated when a situation isn't dealt with immediately. They are quiet and speak in short bursts and are surprisingly quite pleasant to engage with when they have nothing important on their minds, which are often racing with thoughts.

Appearance. Tarius have stunning facial features, with distinct quill-like black feathers protruding from behind their heads and striking featherless red faces. Their bodies, however have mute grey feathers and a strong border to black plumage on their wingtips and exceptionally long legs, which tend to give the Tarius more height over their other Aarakocra cousins.

Size. Tarius reach up to about 5 feet high, with most of it made up by their legs, your size is Medium.

Ability Score Increase. Your Constitution score increases by 1.

Talons. You are proficient with your unarmed strikes using your hands or feet, which deal 1d6 slashing damage on a hit.

Ground Rake. You can move through the space of prone creatures but not end your turn there. If you enter a prone creature's space, you can use your reaction to make a Talon attack against them.

Bounding. Your legs are built for speed, your base movement speed is increased to 35ft.

Orvids

Highly intelligent, stark creatures with a propensity to be overly curious. Their love to harvest arcane lore serendipitously dovetails with an almost unnatural affinity for shiny objects. They have a knack for finding mildly interesting magical trinkets and turn them into personal collections. Orvids are often magic users by consequence of this wanton lust of pretty devices and - despite their wandering eyes and meandering mind - are still considered the smartest of their kind.

Appearance. The head, neck and breast area of Orvids' are glossy black with a metallic green and violet sheen, the wings are black glossed with green or purple striped with white and the legs and bill of the Orvids are black; the iris is dark brown. They are sometimes incorrectly identified as Kenku.

Size. Orvids are the smallest of the Aarakocra and are not much larger than 3 feet high, your size is Small.

Ability Score Increase. Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

Trinket Horder. You start with 2 additional trinkets.

Investigator. You are naturally proficient in the Investigation skill.

Shining Example. You have an eye for finding shiny objects, gaining advantage on trying to find items or objects that can shine or glint in the light within 20 feet of you.

Racial Feats

Wind Walker

A master of the wind, you can pick up subtle changes in the air, able to adapt to sudden changes.
  • Your flight speed is increased by 10 feet.
  • You have advantage on saving throws against effects that involve wind. If you succeed on a saving throw against such an effect, you can use your reaction to immediately move up to half your flying speed.
  • In windy conditions, your flight speed is doubled.

Silent Glide (Bubo Vir Only)

The Bubo Vir are well renowned for their ability to glide silently through the air, leaving nary a wake to detect. Your glide rate is your flight move speed, dropping by 10ft for that distance travelled.
  • Increase your Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • When you glide through the air, you gain advantage on any Dexterity (Stealth) checks related to sound during the day and gain advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks related to both sight and sound during the night. You make no noise while gliding.
  • If you make a melee attack while gliding, if you are hidden from your target, you add an additional 2d4 damage to the attack.

Ground Stomp (Tarius Only)

You are able to use the force from your powerful legs more wisely during combat. You can do the following additional actions during the same Ground Rake movement reaction:
  • As an action you can attempt to stun a prone creature within 5 feet of you by kicking with both feet. Make a talon attack against the target. If you hit, you deal 2d4 + your Strength modifier damage instead of the normal damage for a talon attack and the target must make a Constitution saving throw (DC = 8 + your Strength modifier + your proficiency bonus) or be stunned until the start of your next turn.
  • If you use your Ground Rake trait, you can use it again until the start of your next turn without using your reaction.

Strength of the Alia'eet (Alia'eet Only)

Your flying is strong and powerful compared to other Aarakocra allowing you to carry more weight during flight.
  • Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • You can now fly while wearing medium armor.
  • At a Strength score of 16 you are able to carry a small creature while flying.
  • At a Strength score of 20 you are able to carry a medium creature while flying.
When you are wearing medium armor and carrying a creature, your flight movement is halved.

Beak Performance (Anocetta Only)

As both a bard and aarakocra, a wonderful synergy has emerged between your beak and your musical prowess.
  • Increase your Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • Your beak is considered a musical instrument and can be used as a bard spellcasting focus. As your beak is part of your body, you do not need to spend time to equip or unequip it and leaves both your hands free.

Sky Caster (Orvids Only)

While flying, you feel attuned to the flow of magic, gaining some extra magical benefits as long as you are flying in the air:
  • Any spell with a range greater than touch has its range increased by 30 feet.
  • You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
