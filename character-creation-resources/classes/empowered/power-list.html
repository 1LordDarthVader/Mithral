Astounding Powers

2nd-Level Powers

Aftershock

You can cast Thunderwave by striking the ground at your feet, using Strength as your spellcasting ability, and without using verbal components. The spell's level equals your proficiency bonus. Once you use this power, you must complete a long rest before using it again. In addition, nonmagical, unprotected flames within the spell's area of effect are put out.

Anatomical Armor

While you are not wearing armor, your Armor Class equals 10 + your Dexterity modifier + your Constitution modifier. You can use your Anatomical Armor to determine your AC if the armor you are wearing would leave you with less AC.

Arcane Synesthesia

You can cast Detect Magic at will, without using components.

Barehanded Block

If a creature hits you with a melee attack while you are unarmed, unarmored, and are not wielding a shield, you can use your reaction to add your proficiency bonus to your AC for that attack, potentially causing the attack to miss you.

Boring Monologue

Prerequisite: 12 Charisma
You can cast Charm Person as a 1st level spell, using Charisma as your spellcasting ability. Rather than viewing you as a friendly acquaintance on a failed save, the target will admit that they are swayed by your reasonable line of thinking. Once you use this power, you must complete a short or long rest to use it again.

Collateral Damage

You are proficient in the use of improvised weapons, and you can make use of your Pure Strength feature while wielding an improvised weapon.

Courage

You regain a use of Strength Surge when you score a critical hit with an unarmed strike using Pure Strength against a conscious creature that is at least medium in size.

Destiny

You can cast Augury without using material components or expending spell slots. Once you use this power, you must complete a long rest to do so again.

Daredevil

Prerequisite: 12 Wisdom
You gain 15 feet of Tremorsense, and 5 feet of Blindsight.

Fan Club

Whenever you interact with a non-hostile NPC for the first time, the DM must roll a d20. On a roll of 20, the NPC recognizes you and is a fan of your exploits, and you have advantage on all Charisma checks against them until they have a reason to dislike you.

Members of your Fan Club cannot recognize you while you are using your Secret Identity. However, they can still recognize you even if you have the Nameless power, as they have been theorizing that your death was a hoax.

Harrowing Vision

You can see normally in darkness, both magical and non magical, to a distance of 120 feet.

Immortal Spirit

When you dream or meditate, you can access the memories of empowered beings that came before you. The DM rolls a d20 to determine the quality of the visions you receive, ranging from vague symbolism on a roll of 1, to crucial information on a roll of 20. At the DM's discretion, you might receive visions from one empowered being in particular, and that being might even speak to you directly.

Keen Vision

With clear visibility, you can make out details of extremely distant creatures and objects as small as 2 feet across.

Minor Mentalist

You can cast the Mage Hand and Message cantrips without using components. Your mage hand is invisible.

Momentus Tackle

When you use a bonus action to attempt to grapple or shove a creature, you have an additional 10 feet of movement that you can use exclusively to get close enough to your desired target.

Nameless

When you gain this power, you toss your old identity aside, allowing you to choose a new name and alignment. The old you is presumed dead by those who are not adventuring with you, and you won't be hunted down for crimes the old you committed. If a creature that used to know you discovers that you are not dead, they become stunned until the end of your next turn. You revert to your old identity permanently if you swap this power out for a new one.

Persona

You gain one of the following personas of your choice:

  • Detective. You gain proficiency in the Investigation and Perception skills.
  • Dramatic. You can cast the Thaumaturgy cantrip, and gain proficiency in Performance.
  • Heroic. You gain proficiency in the Insight and Persuasion skills.
  • Mysterious. You gain proficiency in the Stealth and Survival skills.
  • Villainous. You gain proficiency in the Deception and Intimidation skills.

Plot Armor

When you roll a 1 on a d20 for an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll. Once you use this power, you must complete a short or long rest to use it again.

Pure of Heart

You are immune to the charmed condition.

Quipping Expertise

Prerequisite: 12 Charisma
You can cast Vicious Mockery, using Charisma as your spellcasting ability. When a creature fails the saving throw you impose with this spell, it has disadvantage on the next saving throw it makes before the end of its next turn.

Rending Strength

You can expend a use of Strength Surge as an action to crush, bend, or break an inanimate object within your reach that is made of non-magical materials and is not being worn or carried. For larger objects, the area of the object that is bent, broken, or crushed is no larger than a 3-foot cube.

Secret Identity

You have an alter ego: a more mundane identity that you use outside of your adventuring life. Your alter ego has a separate name, home, occupation, and perhaps even a family or a place amongst high society. You can adopt this alternate identity as a disguise, and other creatures believe you to be that person until given an obvious reason not to.

Inexplicably, you can don or doff your alter ego in the blink of an eye, but only when no one is watching.

Sidekick

Prerequisite: 12 Charisma
As an action, you can designate one friendly creature as your sidekick. While you and your sidekick are within 10 feet of one another, each of you can use a reaction to take half of an instance of damage dealt to the other.

Once you choose your sidekick, you cannot choose a new one until you complete a long rest. You can only have one sidekick at a time, so choosing a new sidekick removes the effects of this power from your previous sidekick.

Suplex

When you hit a creature you are grappling with an unarmed strike using Pure Strength, you can move them to the space directly behind you if it is unoccupied, and knock them prone. The creature receives the strike's damage after they are knocked prone.

Unyielding

You can cast the Heroism spell on yourself without using components, using Constitution as your spellcasting ability. Once you use this power, you must complete a long rest to use it again.

Valiant Soul

You are immune to the frightened condition.

"Wham!"

When you score a critical hit with an unarmed strike using Pure Strength, and the target is not prone, you can push the creature back 15 feet and knock them prone.

Wild Leap

Prerequisite: 12 Dexterity
Your jump distance is increased by 5 feet when you make a running long or high jump. You only need to run 5 feet to make a running long or high jump.

Yield

Prerequisite: 12 Charisma
You can cast Command as a 1st level spell, using Charisma as your spellcasting ability. Once you use this power, you cannot use it again until you complete a short or long rest.

5th-Level Powers

Aquatic Nature

Prerequisite: 5th level
You gain 30 feet of swimming speed, and you can breathe underwater. Additionally, you can communicate with beasts and monstrosities with an innate swimming speed.

Cling Grip

Prerequisite: 5th level
While you are not wearing heavy armor, you gain 30 feet of climbing speed. You also gain the ability to move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and upside down along ceilings, while leaving your hands free.

Laser Eyes

Prerequisite: 5th level
You can cast Scorching Ray from your eyes using Constitution as your spellcasting ability and without using components. The spell's level is equal to your proficiency bonus. The spell deals infusion damage if you chose the Conduit Manifestation. Once you use this power, you must complete a long rest to use it again.

Shockwave

Prerequisite: 5th level
As an action on your turn, you can create a shockwave in front of you by punching the air. Creatures in a 15 foot cone in front of you must make a Dexterity saving throw against your power save DC to avoid being knocked prone.

Violence

Prerequisite: 5th level
Whenever you roll a 1 or 2 on your Pure Strength die, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll, even if the new roll is a 1 or a 2.

Wolven-Claw

Prerequisite: 5th level
You can grow claws from your fingertips or between your knuckles as a bonus action. For 1 minute, you gain a bonus to damage rolls for your unarmed strikes made using Pure Strength equal to your proficiency bonus, and your unarmed strikes deal slashing damage. Once you use this power, you must complete a short or long rest to use it again.

7th-Level Powers

Flight

Prerequisite: 7th level
You gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed.

Launching Maneuver

Prerequisite: 7th level
You can expend a use of Strength Surge as a bonus action to propel yourself a number of feet equal to 10 times your proficiency bonus in any direction, leaving a small crater where you were standing. This movement ends early if you impact an obstruction. If you impact a creature, you can attempt to grapple them as part of the same bonus action.

Perfect Landing

Prerequisite: 7th level
You can use your reaction when you fall to reduce any falling damage you take by an amount equal to five times your empowered level.

Vitreous Form

Prerequisite: 7th level
When you are in an area of dim light or darkness, you can use your action to become invisible until you move or take an action or a reaction.

X-Ray Vision

Prerequisite: 7th level
As an action, you gain the ability to see through solid objects to a range of 30 feet. Within that range, you have darkvision if you don't already have it. This special sight lasts for 1 minute or until your concentration ends (as if you were concentrating on a spell). During that time, you perceive objects as ghostly, transparent images. Once you use this power, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

9th-Level Powers

Fixation

Prerequisite: 9th level
You can fixate your willful intent on a creature you can see within 30 feet of you as a bonus action. For 1 minute, so long as you do not damage creatures other than the target, you resist bludgeoning, slashing, and piercing damage from the target. Once you use this power, you must complete a short or long rest to use it again.

Immovable Object

Prerequisite: 9th level
You can expend a use of Strength Surge to concentrate on this power as an action (as if concentrating on a spell). While you maintain concentration and do not move, you cannot be moved against your will by creatures no more than one size larger than you.

When an Empowered using Unstoppable Force pushes an empowered being that is using Immovable Object, both lose concentration and they both take 8d8 force damage.

Noble Sacrifice

Prerequisite: 9th level
Whenever you start your turn with 0 hit points, you can choose a creature that can see you within 30 feet of you. That creature gains Inspiration. Once you use this power, you must complete a long rest to use it again.

Stopping Power

Prerequisite: 9th level
You can expend a use of Strength Surge as a bonus action to become able to grapple or shove creatures of any size for 1 minute. You cannot move while grappling a creature that is two or more sizes larger than you.

Tireless

Prerequisite: 9th level
Whenever you finish a short rest, your exhaustion level, if any, is decreased by 1.

12th-Level Powers

Clothesline

Prerequisite: 12th level
If a creature within 5 feet of you misses you with an attack, you can use a reaction to attempt to grapple or shove them

Healing Factor

Prerequisite: 12th level
So long as you have 1 hit point, you regain hit points equal to a roll of your Pure Strength die at the end of every hour that you spend outside of combat.

Additionally, if you lose a body part, it will grow back and return to full functionality after 1d6 + 1 days if you have at least 1 hit point the whole time.

Maximum Effort

Prerequisite: 12th level
When you expend your last use of Strength Surge, you can make a DC 10 Strength saving throw. On a successful save, you regain a use of Strength Surge. Each time you use this feature after the first, the DC increases by 5. When you finish a short or long rest, the DC resets to 10.

Pure Smite

Prerequisite: 12th level
Whenever you land an unarmed strike using Pure Strength, you can expend as many uses of Strength Surge as you like, with each expended use increasing the strike's damage by one roll of your Pure Strength die.

Unbreakable

Prerequisite: 12th level
You gain resistance to one damage type of your choice.

Unstoppable Force

Prerequisite: 12th level
You can expend a use of Strength Surge to concentrate on this power as an action (as if concentrating on a spell). While you maintain concentration, your movement speed cannot be reduced unless you are incapacitated or killed.

This power does not allow you to walk through obvious obstructions, but it does allow you to push creatures no more than one size larger than you 5 feet away just by walking into them. It also prevents you from being grappled, restrained, or affected by difficult terrain. You can only push the same creature once during each of your turns with this power.

When an Empowered using Unstoppable Force pushes an empowered being that is using Immovable Object, both lose concentration and they both take 8d8 force damage.

15th-Level Powers

Major Mentalist

Prerequisite: 15th level
You can cast either Telepathy or Telekinesis without using material components. Once you use this power, you must complete a long rest to use it again.

Conduit Powers

Dormant Energy

Prerequisite: 7th level, Conduit Manifestation
Whenever you roll for initiative, your Energy Beam automatically becomes charged.

Zephyr Ray

Prerequisite: 9th level, Conduit Manifestation
Your Energy Beam ignores resistance to your infusion damage type. In addition, when you roll damage for your Energy Beam, you can treat any 1 on a damage die as a 2. This also affects your Hyper Beam once you reach 17th level in this class.

Kilojoule

Prerequisite: 12th level, Conduit Manifestation
You gain an additional infusion damage type. If your infusion damage is fire, this new damage type is radiant damage. If your infusion damage is cold, this new damage type is necrotic damage. If your infusion damage is lightning, this new damage type is thunder damage.

Alternatively, you can choose an additional one of the three base infusion damage types (fire, cold, or lightning) to be your second infusion damage type.

Hybrid Powers

Instinctive Reflexes

Prerequisite: 7th level, Hybrid Manifestation
Other creatures don't gain advantage on attack rolls against you as a result of being unseen by you. Additionally, you can cast the Find Traps spell. Once you use this power to cast Find Traps, you must complete a short or long rest to do so again.

Tunneler

Prerequisite: 9th level, Hybrid Manifestation
You gain 5 feet of burrowing speed, allowing you to dig 5 foot diameter tunnels through sand, earth, mud, or ice. You can't burrow through solid rock or metal.

Idol Powers

Guiding Hand

Prerequisite: 5th level, Idol Manifestation
You can cast Guidance, Reassurance, and Resistance without using material components, but only on targets other than yourself.

Zeal

Prerequisite: 9th level, Idol Manifestation
Whenever you embolden a creature with your Exemplar ability, they gain temporary hit points equal to a roll of your Pure Strength die.

Inspired Charge

Prerequisite: 12th level, Idol Manifestation
Creatures can take the Dash action as a bonus action on their turn while emboldened by your Exemplar ability. Once they use their bonus action to do so, they cease to be emboldened.

Mutant Powers

Rotten Shell

Prerequisite: 9th level, Mutant Manifestation
Your unarmed strike can deal acid, poison, or necrotic damage instead of its normal damage type.

Grotesque

Prerequisite: 12th level, Mutant Manifestation
When you use your Monstrous Imitation on a creature, they must make a Wisdom saving throw against your power save DC. On a failed save, the creature is frightened by you for 1 minute. On a successful save, they are frightened by you until the end of your next turn.

Speedster Powers

Bullet-Time

Prerequisite: 7th level, Speedster Manifestation
Using your Super Speed causes ranged attacks targeting you to be made with disadvantage until the start of your next turn.

Lighter than Air

Prerequisite: 9th level, Speedster Manifestation
You gain the ability to move along vertical surfaces and across liquids on your turn without falling during your move.

Super Soldier Powers

Thrill of Battle

Prerequisite: 9th level, Super Soldier Manifestation
When you roll for initiative, you gain temporary hit points equal to your empowered level.

Thousand-Yard Stare

Prerequisite: 12th level, Super Soldier Manifestation
You can't be surprised while you are conscious. Additionally, if a creature knows what you want from them, you can make a Charisma (Intimidation) check against them with advantage simply by maintaining prolonged eye-contact with them.

Titan Powers

Juggernaut

Prerequisite: 5th level, Titan Manifestation
You count as a mount while you are in your True Form, allowing your allies to ride on your back.

Tantrum

Prerequisite: 12th level, Titan Manifestation
If you enter your True Form with no uses of Strength Surge remaining, you regain a single use of Strength Surge.

Vigilante Powers

Equipment Expert

Prerequisite: 5th level, Vigilante Manifestation
You gain proficiency with Thieves' Tools if you don't have it already, and your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make with them.

Ninjutsu

Prerequisite: 7th level, Vigilante Manifestation
Empowered abilities that apply to your unarmed strikes made using Pure Strength also apply to any attack you make with a quarterstaff or dagger, so long as you use Strength as the ability modifier for the attack.
