Psionic Disciplines

Discipline Name Order Effects Description
Adaptive Body Immortal Environmental Adaptation, Adaptive Shield, Energy Adaptation, Energy Immunity You can alter your body to match your surroundings, allowing you to withstand punishing environments. With greater psi energy, you can extend this protection to others.
Aura Sight Awakened Assess Foe, Read Moods, View Aura, Perceive the Unseen You refocus your sight to see the energy that surrounds all creatures. You perceive auras, energy signatures that can reveal key elements of a creature's nature.
Bestial Form Immortal Bestial Claws, Bestial Transformation (Amphibious, Climbing, Flight, Keen Senses, Perfect Senses, Swimming, Tough Hide) You transform your body, gaining traits of different beasts.
Brute Force Immortal Brute Strike, Knock Back, Mighty Leap, Feat of Strength You augment your natural strength with psionic energy, granting you the ability to achieve incredible feats of might.
Celerity Immortal Rapid Step, Agile Defense, Blur of Motion, Surge of Speed, Surge of Action You channel psionic power into your body, honing your reflexes and agility to an incredible degree. The world seems to slow down while you continue to move as normal.
Corrosive Metabolism Immortal Corrosive Touch, Venom Strike, Acid Spray, Breath of the Black Dragon, Breath of the Green Dragon Your control over your body allows you to deliver acid or poison attacks.
Crown of Despair Avatar Crowned in Sorrow, Call to Inaction, Visions of Despair, Dolorous Mind You have learned to harvest seeds of despair in a creature's psyche, wracking it with self-doubt and inaction.
Crown of Disgust Avatar Eye of Horror, Wall of Repulsion, Visions of Disgust, World of Horror You cause a creature to be flooded with emotions of disgust.
Crown of Rage Avatar Primal Fury, Fighting Words, Mindless Courage, Punishing Fury You place a mote of pure fury within a creature's mind, causing its bloodlust to overcome its senses and for it to act as you wish it to.
Diminution Immortal Miniature Form, Toppling Shift, Sudden Shift, Microscopic Form You manipulate the matter that composes your body, drastically reducing your size without surrendering any of your might.
Giant Growth Immortal Ogre Form, Giant Form You infuse yourself with psionic energy to grow to tremendous size, bolstering your strength and durability.
Intellect Fortress Awakened Psychic Backlash, Psychic Parry, Psychic Redoubt You forge an indomitable wall of psionic energy around your mind - one that allows you to launch counterattacks against your opponents.
Iron Durability Immortal Iron Hide, Steel Hide, Iron Resistance You transform your body to become a living metal, allowing you to shrug off attacks that would cripple weaker creatures.
Mantle of Awe Avatar Charming Presence, Center of Attention, Invoke Awe You learn to use psionic energy to manipulate others with a subtle combination of psi and your own, natural charm.
Mantle of Command Avatar Coordinated Movement, Commander's Sight, Command to Strike, Strategic Mind, Overwhelming Attack You exert an aura of trust and authority, enhancing the coordination among your allies.
Mantle of Courage Avatar Incite Courage, Aura of Victory, Pillar of Confidence You focus your mind on courage, radiating confidence and bravado to your allies.
Mantle of Fear Avatar Incite Fear, Unsettling Aura, Incite Panic You tap into a well of primal fear and turn yourself into a beacon of terror to your enemies.
Mantle of Fury Avatar Incite Fury, Mindless Charge, Aura of Bloodletting, Overwhelming Fury You allow the primal fury lurking deep within your mind to burst forth, catching you and your allies in an implacable bloodthirst.
Mantle of Joy Avatar Soothing Presence, Comforting Aura, Aura of Jubilation, Beacon of Recovery You tap into the joy within you, radiating it outward in soothing, psychic energy that brings hope and comfort to creatures around you.
Mastery of Air Wu Jen Wind Step, Wind Stream, Cloak of Air, Wind Form, Misty Form, Animate Air You become one with the power of elemental air.
Mastery of Fire Wu Jen Combustion, Rolling Flame, Detonation, Fire Form, Animate Fire You align your mind with the energy of elemental fire.
Mastery of Force Wu Jen Push, Move, Inertial Armor, Telekinetic Barrier, Grasp, Crush As a student of psionic power, you perceive the potential energy that flows through all things. You reach out with your mind, transforming the potential into the actual. Objects and creatures move at your command.
Mastery of Ice Wu Jen Ice Spike, Ice Sheet, Frozen Sanctuary, Frozen Rain, Ice Barrier You master the power of ice, shaping it to meet you demands.
Mastery of Light and Darkness Wu Jen Darkness, Light, Shadow Beasts, Radiant Beam You claim dominion over light and darkness with your mind.
Mastery of Water Wu Jen Desiccate, Watery Grasp, Water Whip, Water Breathing, Water Sphere, Animate Water Your mind becomes one with elemental water, attuning your thoughts to its ebb and flow.
Mastery of Weather Wu Jen Cloud Steps, Hungry Lightning, Wall of Clouds, Whirlwind, Lightning Leap, Wall of Thunder, Thunder Clap Your mind reaches into the sky, reshaping the stuff of storms to serve your needs.
Mastery of Wood and Earth Wu Jen Animate Weapon, Warp Weapon, Warp Armor, Wall of Wood, Armored Form, Animate Earth You attune your mind to seize control of wood and earth.
Nomadic Arrow Nomad Speed Dart, Seeking Missile, Faithful Archer You imbue a ranged weapon with a strange semblance of sentience, allowing it to unerringly find its mark.
Nomadic Chameleon Nomad Chameleon, Step from Sight, Enduring Invisibility You create a screen of psychic power that distorts your appearance, allowing you to blend into the background or even turn invisible.
Nomadic Mind Nomad Wandering Mind, Find Creature, Item Lore, Psychic Speech, Wandering Eye, Phasing Eye You dispatch part of your psyche into the noosphere, the collective vista of minds and knowledge possessed by living things.
Nomadic Step Nomad Step of a Dozen Paces, Nomadic Anchor, Defensive Step, There and Back Again, Transposition, Baleful Transposition, Phantom Caravan, Nomad's Gate You exert your mind on the area around you, twisting the intraplanar pathways you perceive to allow instantaneous travel.
Precognition Awakened Precognitive Hunch, All-Around Sight, Danger Sense, Victory Before Battle By analyzing information around you, from subtle hints to seemingly disconnected facts, you learn to weave a string of probabilities in an instant that gives you extraordinary insights.
Psionic Restoration Immortal Mend Wounds, Restore Health, Restore Life, Restore Vigor You wield psionic energy to cure wounds and restore health to yourself and others.
Psionic Weapon Immortal Ethereal Weapon, Lethal Strike, Augmented Weapon You have learned how to channel psionic energy into your attacks, lending them devastating power.
Psychic Assault Awakened Psionic Blast, Ego Whip, Id Insinuation, Psychic Blast, Psychic Crush You wield your mind like a weapon, unleashing salvos of psionic energy.
Psychic Disruption Awakened Distracting Haze, Daze, Mind Storm You create psychic static that disrupts other creatures' ability to think clearly.
Psychic Inquisition Awakened Hammer of Inquisition, Forceful Query, Ransack Mind, Phantom Idea You reach into a creature's mind to uncover information or plant ideas within it.
Telepathic Contact Awakened Exacting Query, Occluded Mind, Broken Will, Psychic Grip, Psychic Domination By channeling psionic power, you gain the ability to control other creatures by substituting your will for their own.
Third Eye Nomad Tremorsense, Unwavering Eye, Piercing Sight, Truesight You create a third, psychic eye in your mind, which you cast out into the world. It channels thoughts and knowledge back to you, greatly enhancing your senses.
