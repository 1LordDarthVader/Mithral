Strength Domain

Gods of strength grant their faithful power and athletic ability, but realize one crucial detail. Strength can't be taught. It must be built through practice and training. These gods watch over their faithful and encourage them to observe and emulate their indomitable strength, for though they will never equal it, they can aspire to mimicry. They invite the faithful to scrutinize every move and practice what they see.
Strength Domain Spells
Cleric Level Spells
1st Divine Favor, Shield of Faith
3rd Enhance Ability, Protection From Poison
5th Haste, Protection From Energy
7th Gift of Vivacity, Stoneskin
9th Destructive Wave, Fissure

Bonus Proficiency

At 1st level, you gain proficiency with heavy armor.

Acolyte of Strength

At 1st level, you learn one cantrip of your choice from the druid spell list, and it is considered a cleric spell for you.

In addition, you gain proficiency in one of the following skills of your choice: Animal Handling, Athletics, Nature, or Survival.

Channel Divinity: Feat of Strength

At 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to enhance your physical might. When you make an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw using Strength, you can use your Channel Divinity to gain a +10 bonus to the roll. You make this choice after you see the roll, but before the DM says whether the roll succeeds or fails.

Channel Divinity: Titan's Blessing

At 6th level, when a creature within 30 feet of you makes an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw using Strength, you can use your reaction to grant that creature a +10 bonus to the roll, using your Channel Divinity. You make this choice after you see the roll, but before the DM says whether the roll succeeds or fail.

Divine Strike

At 8th level, you gain the ability to infuse your weapon strikes with divine energy. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can cause the attack to deal an extra 1d8 damage of the same type dealt by the weapon to the target. When you reach 14th level, the extra damage increases to 2d8.

Avatar of Battle

At 17th level, you gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks.
