Universal Features

Extra Feat

Starting at 1st level, and again at 4th level, you gain additional skill in one aspect of adventuring. You gain one feat of your choice.

Genius

Starting at 1st level, and again at 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th levels, you can apply your intelligence to every situation. You gain a number of Genius points equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 0). If you have any levels in the Mystic or Savant classes, are a Blood Hunter or Mentor who uses Intelligence as their primary ability, or are a spellcaster who uses Intelligence as their spellcasting ability, you gain half as many points as you otherwise would.

Whenever you gain a level, you can spend Genius points to gain the following benefits of your choice.
Benefit Cost
Proficiency in one skill of your choice 2
Expertise in a skill with which you have proficiency 3
Proficiency in one language of your choice 1
Proficiency with one tool of your choice 1
Expertise with a tool with which you have proficiency 2
Proficiency in light armor 2
Proficiency in medium armor 3
Proficiency in heavy armor 4
Proficiency with shields 2
Proficiency with one simple weapon of your choice 1
Proficiency with one martial weapon of your choice 2
Proficiency with one exotic weapon of your choice 3

Grit

Starting at 5th level, you can push on where others would falter, but at the cost of your own life. When you drop to 0 hit points but are not killed outright, at any time before the start of your next turn, you may declare Grit.

For the next minute, you remain conscious regardless of what affects you. No healing will work on you during this time. After this minute elapses, you die.

Starting at 11th level, if you would be killed outright, you may instead immediately declare Grit.
