Path of the Titan

Giants are the stuff of legend, enormous berserkers who inspire a primal fear in mortals. Barbarians who follow the path of the titan use ancient giant magic to grow to extraordinary sizes.

Titan-Sized

Starting at 3rd level, when you rage you can also grow. Using the same bonus action you use to enter a rage, you can grow to the size of a Large creature. While enlarged, you gain the following benefits:
  • Your size doubles in all dimensions and your weight is multiplied by eight.
  • Your melee attacks deal an extra 1d4 damage.
  • You can use this feature a number of times equal to half of your maximum rages (rounding up), and regain all expended uses on a long rest.

Runic Armor

Starting at 6th level, the giant magic within you grants you improved defense. You gain resistance to a type of damage determined by the type of giant you choose below.
Giant Type Damage Type
Storm Lightning
Cloud Thunder
Fire Fire
Frost Cold
Stone Acid
Hill Poison

Giant's Grasp

Starting at 10th level, your grasp can challenge that even of a true giant. You can grapple any creature, regardless of size, and can drag them at no penalty to your movement.

Giant-Sized

Starting at 14th level, you can temporarily grow even larger. You can expend two uses of your Titan-Sized feature at once to grow to the size of a Huge creature. While enlarged, you gain the following benefits:
  • Your size quadruples in all dimensions and your weight is multiplied by sixty-four.
  • Your melee attacks deal an extra 2d4 damage.
  • Your unarmed strikes deal 1d10 bludgeoning damage.
