Runes

ᛉ Algiz - Protection

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you (you can choose yourself). The chosen creature gains temporary hit points equal to 5 times the level of the spell slot expended, or 7 times if the spell cast was of the abjuration school.

ᚨ Ansuz - Understanding

When you invoke this rune, you and creatures of your choice within 30 feet of you can communicate telepathically for a number of minutes equal to half the level of spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of 1), or equal to the level of spell slot if the spell cast was of the divination school.

ᛒ Berkana - Companionship

When you invoke this rune, you gain a familiar as if with the Find Familiar spell for a number of minutes equal to the level of spell slot expended, or twice the level if the spell cast was of the conjuration school.

ᛞ Dagaz - Illumination

When you invoke this rune, you radiate for the next minute. You radiate bright light in a radius equal to 5 times the level of spell slot expended, or 10 times if the spell cast was of the divination school, and dim light the same distance beyond that. The light is sunlight. You can stop radiating at any time as a bonus action.

ᛖ Ehwaz - Movement

When you invoke this rune, your speed increases by a number of feet equal to 5 times the level of the spell slot expended, or 7 times if the spell cast was of the transposition school, and your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks. These benefits last until the start of your next turn.

ᛇ Eihwaz - Death

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The chosen target takes necrotic damage equal to 5 times the level of spell slot expended, or 7 times if the spell cast was of the necromancy school.

ᚠ Fehu - Security

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, if the chosen creature fails a saving throw, they can choose to reroll it and must use the new roll. The chosen creature can do this once, or twice if the spell cast was of the abjuration school.

ᚷ Gebo - Balance

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, the chosen target can benefit from neither advantage nor disadvantage.

ᚺ Hagalaz - Wrath

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, saving throws the chosen creature makes gain a penalty equal to half the level of the spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of +1), or equal to the level of spell slot expended if the spell cast was of the evocation school.

ᛜ Inguz - Growth

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The chosen creature is enlarged as if the Enlarge/Reduce spell was cast on them for a number of rounds equal to half the level of spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of 1), or equal to the level of spell slot expended if the spell cast was of the transmutation school.

ᛁ Isa - Stasis

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The chosen creature must make a Strength saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed saving throw, the target is restrained for a number of rounds equal to the level of spell slot expended, or twice the level if the spell cast was of the enchantment school. An affected target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

ᛃ Jera - Harvest

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. If the chosen creature dies within the next minute, you regain the expended spell slot.

ᚲ Kennaz - Vision

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, the chosen creature gains truesight out to a number of feet equal to 5 times the level of the spell slot expended, or 10 times if the spell cast was of the divination school.

ᛚ Lagaz - Emotions

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The chosen creature must make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed saving throw, the target is frightened of you for a number of rounds equal to the level of spell slot expended, or twice the level if the spell cast was of the enchantment school. An affected target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

ᛗ Mannaz - Help

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, saving throws the chosen creature makes gain a bonus equal to half the level of the spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of +1), or equal to the level of spell slot expended if the spell cast was of the abjuration school.

ᚾ Nauthiz - Restriction

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, ability checks the chosen creature makes gain a penalty equal to half the level of the spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of +1), or equal to the level of spell slot expended if the spell cast was of the enchantment school.

ᛟ Othala - Experience

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, ability checks the chosen creature makes gain a bonus equal to half the level of the spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of +1), or equal to the level of spell slot expended if the spell cast was of the divination school.

ᛈ Perthro - Chance

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, if the chosen creature fails an attack roll, they can choose to reroll it and must use the new roll. The chosen creature can do this once, or twice if the spell cast was of the evocation school.

ᚱ Raidho - Travel

When you invoke this rune, you teleport up to a number of feet equal to 10 times the level of the spell slot expended, or 15 times if the spell cast was of the transposition school.

ᛊ Sowilo - Cleansing

When you invoke this rune, choose a creature you can see within 30 feet of you. The chosen creature can immediately make saving throws against a number of effects affecting it equal to half the level of spell slot expended, or equal to the level if the spell cast was of the restoration school.

ᚦ Thurisaz - Regeneration

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you (you can choose yourself). The chosen creature regains lost hit points equal to twice the level of spell slot expended, or three times if the spell cast was of the restoration school.

ᛏ Tiwaz - Battle

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, attack rolls that target the chosen creature gain a bonus equal to half the level of the spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of +1), or equal to the level of spell slot expended if the spell cast was of the evocation school.

ᚢ Uruz - Strength

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, attack rolls the chosen creature makes gain a bonus equal to half the level of the spell slot expended (rounded up, minimum of +1), or equal to the level of spell slot expended if the spell cast was of the evocation school.

ᚹ Wunjo - Success

When you invoke this rune, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of you. Until the end of your next turn, if the chosen creature fails an ability check, they can choose to reroll it and must use the new roll. The chosen creature can do this once, or twice if the spell cast was of the divination school.
