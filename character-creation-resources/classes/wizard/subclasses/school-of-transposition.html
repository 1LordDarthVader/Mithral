School of Transposition

Wizards of the school of transposition, called transposers, learn magic related to teleportation and planar travel. Most planeswalkers have some experience in this school.

Transposition Savant

Starting when you select this school at 2nd level, the gold and time you must spend to copy a transposition spell into your spellbook is halved.

Telekinetic

Also starting at 2nd level, you can lift objects through magical force alone. You learn the Mage Hand cantrip, and the spectral hand is invisible when you cast it.

If you already know the mage hand cantrip, you learn another cantrip of your choice from the arcane spell list.

Benign Transportation

Starting at 6th level, you can use your action to teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see. Alternatively, you can choose a space within range that is occupied by a Small or Medium creature. If that creature is willing, you both teleport, swapping places.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest or you cast a transposition spell of 1st level or higher.

Blink Teleport

Starting at 10th level, you can blink around the battlefield, teleporting rapidly. As a bonus action, you can expend 15 feet of your movement to teleport up to 10 feet away. You can use this feature once per turn.

In addition, if your speed is currently reduced to 0, you may use your action to use this feature without expending movement.

Planeswalker

Starting at 14th level, you can transport yourself across planes with ease. You can cast the Plane Shift spell without expending a spell slot. When cast in this way, the spell does not require material components, cannot transport unwilling creatures, and takes ten minutes to cast.

You can use this feature once, and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest.

In addition, you have advantage on ability checks made to recall information about the outer planes or their inhabitants.
