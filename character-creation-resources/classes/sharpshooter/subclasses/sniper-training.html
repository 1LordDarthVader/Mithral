Sniper Training

Calm, patient, hidden, deadly. The attributes of any sniper. Snipers are able to take down powerful targets from far away before they are even seen with high powered rifles and extreme accuracy.

Marksman

Starting at 3rd level, you have trained extensively with using ranged weapons at extreme ranges. You gain the following benefits:
  • Attacking at long range doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged weapon attack rolls.
  • When you are in cover, you gain advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.

Covering Fire

Also starting at 3rd level, you use your position to cover your allies from enemy fire. When a creature you can see is attacked, if the attacker is within the range of a ranged weapon you are holding, you can use your reaction to make a single ranged weapon attack against the attacker.

Deadeye

Starting at 7th level, when you hit a crucial spot on a creature they suffer extensively. You can roll one additional weapon damage die when determining the extra damage for a critical hit with a ranged attack.

Expert Marksman

Starting at 10th level, you have prefected finding the best place to set up to cover your allies. You gain the following benefits:
  • Your ranged weapon attacks ignore half and three-quarters cover.
  • As a bonus action, you give yourself advantage on your next attack roll on the current turn. You can use this bonus action only if you haven't moved during this turn, and after you use the bonus action, your speed is 0 until the end of the current turn.

Expert Deadeye

Starting at 15th level, you can roll two additional weapon damage dice when determining the extra damage for a critical hit with a ranged attack.

Lethal Precision

Starting at 18th level, you score a critical hit when you roll a 19 or a 20 on ranged attack rolls.
