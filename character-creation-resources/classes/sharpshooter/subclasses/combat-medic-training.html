Combat Medic Training

Combat medics are those who keep their allies alive and fighting. Armed with medicine and bandages, they treat their friends' wounds in the midst of the battlefield while defending them from the enemy onslaught.

Field Medic

Starting at 3rd level, you have been trained in administering first aid in the midst of battle. You are gain proficiency in the Medicine skill.

In addition, you gain the ability to heal a creature quickly in a pinch. You gain a Medicine Die, which is a d8. As an action, you can touch a willing creature within 5 feet of you. The target regains hit points equal to a roll of your Medicine Die plus your Wisdom modifier.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

Close Quarters Combat Training

Also at 3rd level, you have received extensive training in fighting at small distances. When you are wielding a ranged weapon, you do not suffer disadvantage as a result of being within 5 feet of a hostile creature.

In addition, when you make an opportunity attack, you can make a ranged weapon attack against the target.

Defend the Wounded

Starting at 7th level, your devotion to the wounded gives you clarity in battle. While you are within 5 feet of a friendly creature who is below half their hit point maximum, you gain advantage on attack rolls.

Field Surgeon

Starting at 10th level, your experience in field medicine has built upon your knowledge. When you use your Field Medic feature, you can expend a number of uses up to half your proficiency bonus (rounded down), adding a roll of your Medicine Die to the healing for each use expended past the first.

In addition your Medicine Die turns into a d10.

Medic's Fury

Starting at 15th level, while you are under the effects of your Defend the Wounded feature, your weapon attacks deal extra damage equal to your Wisdom modifier.

In addition, if you use your Field Medic feature, you can make 1 weapon attack as a bonus action.

Field Hospital

Starting at 18th level, you maximize your training and experience for the treatment of the injured. You gain the following benefits:
  • You double your proficiency bonus for checks made with your Medicine proficiency.
  • When you use your Field Medic feature, you can add your proficiency bonus to the amount of hit points you restore.
  • Tou can expend a use of your Field Medic feature to end one disease or condition affecting the target instead of restoring hit points.
  • Your Medicine Die turns into a d12.
