Weapon Master

You have practiced extensively with a variety of weapons, gaining the following benefits:
  • Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • You gain proficiency with three weapons of your choice. Each one must be a simple or a martial weapon. If you are already proficient with a weapon, you instead gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls with that weapon.
