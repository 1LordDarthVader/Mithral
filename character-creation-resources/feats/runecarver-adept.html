Runecarver Adept

Prerequisite: the ability to cast at least one spell

You have studied runes of ancient giant languages. You learn two Runes of your choice.

When you cast a spell using a spell slot, you can invoke one of the runes you know.

You can invoke no more than one rune per spell. You can use this feature a number of times equal to half your proficiency bonus (rounded down, minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
