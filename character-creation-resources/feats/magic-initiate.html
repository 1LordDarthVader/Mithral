Magic Initiate

Choose a class: bard, cleric, druid, harbinger, pale master, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard. You learn two cantrips of your choice from that class's spell list.

In addition, choose one 1st-level spell to learn from that same list. Using this feat, you can cast the spell once at its lowest level, and you must finish a long rest before you can cast it in this way again.

Your spellcasting ability for these spells depends on the class you chose. Choose it from among the options available to the class chosen, as listed below:
Class Choices
Bard Wisdom or Charisma
Cleric Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma
Druid Intelligence or Wisdom
Harbinger Intelligence or Wisdom
Pale Master Intelligence or Wisdom
Sorcerer Constitution, Wisdom, or Charisma
Warlock Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma
Wizard Intelligence or Wisdom
