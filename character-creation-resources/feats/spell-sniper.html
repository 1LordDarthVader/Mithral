Spell Sniper

Prerequisite: The ability to cast at least one spell
You have learned techniques to enhance your attacks with certain kinds of spells, gaining the following benefits:
  • When you cast a spell that requires you to make an attack roll, the spell's range is doubled.
  • Your ranged spell attacks ignore half cover and three-quarters cover.
  • You learn one cantrip that requires an attack roll. Choose the cantrip from the bard, cleric, druid, harbinger, pale master, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard spell list. Your spellcasting ability for this cantrip depends on the spell list you chose from. Choose it from among the options available to the class chosen, as listed below:
Class Choices
Bard Wisdom or Charisma
Cleric Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma
Druid Intelligence or Wisdom
Harbinger Intelligence or Wisdom
Pale Master Intelligence or Wisdom
Sorcerer Constitution, Wisdom, or Charisma
Warlock Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma
Wizard Intelligence or Wisdom
