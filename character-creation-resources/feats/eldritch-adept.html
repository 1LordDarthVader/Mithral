Eldritch Adept

Prerequisite: the ability to cast at least one spell
Studying occult lore, you have unlocked eldritch power within yourself: you learn one Eldritch Invocation option of your choice from the warlock class, assuming you meet its prerequisites. Whenever you gain a level, you can replace the invocation with another one from the warlock class.
