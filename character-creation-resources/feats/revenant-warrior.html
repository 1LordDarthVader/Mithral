Revenant Warrior

You have trained extensively with double weapons. You gain the following benefits:
  • Increase your Strength or Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • You gain proficiency with one weapon of your choice that has the Double property.
  • While wielding weapon with the Double property with two hands, you gain +1 AC.
