Mighty Summons

Prerequisite: the ability to cast at least one spell
You have mastered summoning minions to fight for you, and your magic leaks over into their abilities. Creatures you summon by casting spells gain the following benefits:
  • Their attacks are considered magical for the purposes of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical damage.
  • They gain temporary hit points equal to your level when you summon them.
