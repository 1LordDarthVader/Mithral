Unbreakable

You have trained yourself, body, mind, and soul, to become unbreakable in the face of all adversity, gaining the following benefits:
  • Increase your Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.
  • You gain resistance to one damage type of your choice from the following list: acid, cold, fire, force, lightning, necrotic, psychic, poison, radiant, or thunder.
You can take this feat multiple times, and must choose a different damage type each time.
